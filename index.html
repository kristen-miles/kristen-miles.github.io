Supplementary Curriculum Creation and Implementation
This project focused on developing resources to help beginning learners master their letters and sounds. As the project manager, I coordinated the creation of lesson plans, worksheets, flashcards, interactive activities, and other resources designed to make learning letters and sounds engaging and accessible. These materials were carefully aligned with the curriculum to provide targeted support for students at the early stages of reading. These materials are now being used in apporoximately 40 elementary schools in the Salt Lake Valley.
University and Elementary Partnership
The alumni partnership project involved universities donating university-branded items to a high-poverty school. As the project manager, I coordinated the donation of these items, such as shirts, bags, or other branded merchandise, to help build school spirit and connect students with higher education. While the support may have been limited to material donations, it helped to raise awareness about college and create a sense of possibility for students.
